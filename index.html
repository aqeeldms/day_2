Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Caranberries, and Brie

Wow your tase buds with this festive foccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized oninons, tart carnberries, and cremy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instantyeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
